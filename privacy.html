Privacy Policy

This page explains how information is handled when you visit wymingdigital.co.uk.

1. Overview

Wyming Digital is a small informational website. We do not actively collect personal data, run analytics, or display advertising. However, some data may be processed automatically when you visit this site or interact with third-party content.

2. Automatically Collected Technical Data

When you access this website, basic technical information may be processed by our hosting provider for operational and security purposes. This can include:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Date and time of access

This data is not used to personally identify visitors and is not combined with other data sources.

3. Third-Party Content (GitHub)

This website links to and embeds content from GitHub (for example, repository previews or embedded code views).

When pages containing GitHub content are loaded, your browser may connect directly to GitHub’s servers. As a result, GitHub may receive technical data such as your IP address and browser information.

This processing is necessary to display the embedded content. Wyming Digital has no control over how GitHub processes this data.

GitHub’s privacy policy can be found here:
GitHub Privacy Statement

4. Cookies

Wyming Digital does not set cookies for analytics, advertising, or tracking purposes.

Third-party services you interact with (such as GitHub) may use cookies according to their own policies.

5. Contacting Us

If you choose to contact us via an email link on this website (*@wymingdigital.co.uk), any information you provide will only be used to respond to your enquiry and will not be shared with third parties.

6. Your Rights

Under UK data protection law, you may have rights including the right to access, correct, or request deletion of personal data. If you have questions about privacy on this site, please get in touch using the contact details provided on the website.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the website changes or if legal requirements change. The current version will always be available on this page.
